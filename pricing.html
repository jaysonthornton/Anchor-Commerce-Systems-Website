Pricing FAQ

Is hosting required?

Hosting and management is recommended so Anchor can keep the website maintained, backed up, monitored, and updated.

What counts as minor content updates?

Examples include changing hours, swapping photos, updating text, editing contact info, or making small page updates. Larger redesigns or new features are quoted separately.

Is POS available right now?

Anchor POS is currently in development. Websites, logo design, and hosting are the first services being offered publicly.

Can the website support ordering later?

Yes. Anchor websites are being built with future ordering and business system integrations in mind.
